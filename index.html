What is the physics behind this?

Physics formula

Friction formula :

$$F_f = m{v^2 \over R}$$

$$\mu N = \mu mg = m{v^2 \over R}$$

$$\mu = {v^2 \over gR}$$

Lean Angle formula :

$$ \tau_{net-0} = 0 = mgL \sin \theta - m{v^2 \over r }L \cos \theta $$

$$g \sin \theta = {v^2 \over r} \cos \theta$$

$${\sin \theta \over \cos \theta} = \tan \theta = {v^2 \over rg}$$

$$\tan \theta = \mu $$

What does that means ?
